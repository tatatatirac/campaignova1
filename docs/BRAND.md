# Campaignova brand system

## Brand

**Name:** Campaignova  
**Category:** AI Marketing Director  
**Primary promise:** A complete monthly marketing system that tells a business
what to publish, when to publish it and why it should work.

Campaignova combines `campaign` with `nova`: a new campaign, a new burst of
momentum and a brighter point of direction.

## Positioning

Campaignova is not an AI text generator. It is the operating layer between a
business strategy and monthly marketing execution.

### One-line description

Your AI Marketing Director for strategy, content and ready-to-publish campaigns.

### Primary headline

Know exactly what to post next.

### Primary CTA

Build my marketing plan

### Supporting messages

- One business profile becomes a complete 30-day marketing system.
- Strategy first; content follows approved decisions.
- Ready-made videos arrive on a recommended local release calendar.
- The customer owns the output and may download entitled videos at any time.

## Voice

- decisive, not loud;
- expert, not academic;
- commercially direct, not hype-driven;
- concise, but never vague;
- optimistic without promising guaranteed results.

Avoid:

- “revolutionary” and empty AI superlatives;
- guaranteed revenue or guaranteed campaign success;
- describing the product as a prompt or content generator;
- technical implementation language in customer-facing copy.

## Visual identity

### Logo

The primary mark is a forward-slash campaign symbol inside a rounded dark
square. It represents:

- a new campaign starting;
- movement from plan to execution;
- a rising trajectory;
- the letter `C` reduced to a directional mark.

The wordmark is `Campaignova`, with `AI Marketing Director` used as the product
descriptor where space allows.

### Colors

- Campaign Ink: `#121412`
- Campaign Paper: `#F6F4EE`
- Nova Lime: `#D9FF43`
- Direction Violet: `#695CFF`
- Muted Copy: `#666B64`

Nova Lime is used for action and release-ready states. Direction Violet is used
for guidance, progress and strategic system states.

### Typography

- Display: Georgia until the final licensed webfont is selected
- Interface/body: Arial until the final licensed webfont is selected

The production font decision is intentionally deferred until brand ownership
and the domain are confirmed.
