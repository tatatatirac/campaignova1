# Campaignova — MVP specification

## Product promise

Campaignova, the AI Marketing Director, turns a company profile into an actionable 30-day
marketing system: decisions, content, emails, landing-page copy and ready-made
vertical videos delivered on a recommended publishing schedule.

It is not positioned as an AI text generator. It is positioned as a marketing
director that decides what to say, where to say it and when to publish it.

## Primary customer

Small service businesses and owner-led companies that:

- do not have an internal marketing director;
- need a predictable monthly content system;
- want ready-to-use output instead of prompts;
- can justify a monthly spend of $29–$149.

## MVP user journey

1. Visitor sees the offer and a concrete sample output.
2. Visitor creates an account or joins the controlled beta.
3. User completes a three-step company onboarding.
4. System saves the company profile as Business Memory.
5. User starts a monthly marketing plan.
6. Strategy is generated first and presented for confirmation.
7. Content sections are generated from the approved strategy.
8. User receives a calendar, posts, emails, landing copy and video releases.
9. User follows the recommended schedule or explicitly unlocks all videos.
10. Next month reuses Business Memory and creates a fresh plan.

## Generated package

- executive summary;
- positioning and core offer;
- customer profile;
- campaign objective and key message;
- channel strategy;
- 30-day content calendar;
- 30 platform-ready posts;
- 10 short-video scripts;
- 5-email campaign;
- landing-page copy;
- sales pitch;
- KPI targets and recommended budget;
- package-specific ready-made MP4 videos.

## Video delivery

- Starter: 1 video available immediately.
- Growth: 5 videos; first immediately, four released by calendar.
- Director: 30 videos; first immediately, remaining videos released by calendar.
- Every video has a clean preview without a third-party watermark.
- Customer logo is optional and controlled by the customer.
- Every release displays local publishing date, time, caption, CTA and platform.
- Customers may unlock all entitled videos after acknowledging that deviating
  from the campaign schedule can affect results.

## Pricing

### Starter — $29/month

- one brand;
- one monthly plan;
- 30 posts;
- 10 video scripts;
- one ready-made MP4 video;
- five section regenerations;
- text export.

### Growth — $79/month

- one brand;
- two monthly plans;
- five ready-made MP4 videos;
- Business Memory;
- branded PDF export;
- 20 section regenerations;
- KPI plan.

### Director — $149/month

- up to three brands;
- five monthly plans;
- 30 ready-made MP4 videos;
- competitor analysis;
- advanced campaign variants;
- PowerPoint export;
- priority processing.

Growth is the default highlighted plan.

## Explicitly excluded from MVP

- publishing directly to social networks;
- true multi-agent orchestration;
- unrestricted generation;
- custom AI video generation;
- PowerPoint export;
- complex team permissions;
- Stripe billing before the US LLC and Stripe account are ready.

## Success criteria for private beta

- 10–20 completed company onboardings;
- at least 70% of users generate a full plan;
- at least 40% return to view the release calendar;
- at least five documented customer outcomes;
- sufficient willingness to pay to activate monthly subscriptions.
